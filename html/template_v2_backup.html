How to use this Camere-Ready Verification Tool
  • Navigation Click on flaw number in the "Flaw Navigation" to jump to the corresponding location in both papers.
  • Default Highlight All flaws in submitted paper and addressed flaws in camera-ready paper are highlighted in neutral gray color.
  • Yellow Highlight Once you click a flaw in the "Flaw Navigation", it is highlighted in yellow color.
  • Red flaw numbers indicate issues not addressed in the camera ready paper.
  • Highlighted Text Click When you click the highlighted text in either of the papers, you are shown details about the flaw or addressed flaw, which you can review.
  • Survey In the detailed view of flaw or addressed flaw, you are given a short survey to click-fill. You can do this for all the flaws and addressed flaws and download the complete survey from the "Survey Progress" panel.
